Tutorial

This game is based on the mobile game "Reigns"

The goal of the game is to survive as long as you can.

To survive you must balance your hunger, health, and sanity.

You will be met with scenarios and must make a choice in each.

The choices you make will affect your stats. Don't let them fall to zero!

Even having too much of a stat may be detrimental, so try to balance your stats.
